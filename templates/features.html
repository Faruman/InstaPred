Our AI
So, this is how the magic works:
Using dedicated scraping scripts we gather massive amounts of Instagram pictures as well as information related to the post and store them in our designated database in the cloud. Now comes the interesting part: Using advanced machine learning technique we train a Neural Network that finds patterns in pictures that correspond to a high number of likes. When uploading a picture, the trained model screens your picture and looks for the mentioned patterns. Doing this we are able to accurately predict how many likes your post will get.
